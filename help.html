    2. Editing: Lyrics & Chords

    This is the most important function of the app. You change mode with the  C / L-button in the top menu.

      C - Chord Mode:

      This is the default mode. Here the editor is "smart" and helps you with chords. Chords sticks to text. If there's no text, wright ...... ..... and place your chord.

    Create chords: By clicking on a word in the text, the chord selector opens. The button in the center is for stating the chord. Inner circle - Choose "A, B, C..." Middle circle is for coloring the chord m, m7... Finaly the outer circle is for bass note /C /G ...



    Create Chords Manually in L Mode

    Type [G7] any where in your lyrics. Save your song when you're done. The next time you open the project the chord is in the right formate

    • Edit Chord: Click on the blue handle on an existing chord to change it. (Hover over the chord to make the handle appear)

    • Move Chord: Click and drag the small blue handle on a chord to move it.

    • Drag-Copy Chord (Computer): Hold down [alt], click and drag the small handle to the desired position.

      L - Lyrics Mode:

      In this mode, the editor works like a regular text editor. You cannot click on words to create chords. This mode is perfect when you just want to type in long pieces of text without accidentally creating chords.

    3. Sections (Verse, Chorus, etc.)

    Add structure your song:

    1. Place the text cursor on the line you want verse, chorus, to end up above

    2. Open the sida bar menu ‚ò∞ > Sections.

    3. Select your section marker from the drop-down menu and click Insert.

    4. The line will now show "Verse" in larger text size and the lyric section will move to the line below.

      5. Change marker

      Place the text cursor on the text line under the section marker you want to change. Go to "Sections" and choose a new marker. Click on "Insert" and you're done.

       

      Remove marker

      Dubble click/tap on the section marker and its gone.

    7. Import / Export

    You can easily backup and share your songs.

      Export:

      • PDF: Creates a nice PDF of a song

      • TXT: Saves the song as a plain text file.

      • Song->JSON: Saves the current song in a special format that the app can read again. Use this for backup!

      • All->JSON: Saves all your songs in a single file. Perfect for total backup.

      • ZIP: Saves all your songs as separate PDF files in a ZIP file.

      Import:

      • Imp.->JSON Select a .json file (that you previously exported) to load one or more songs. New songs you've made after exporting to .json-file will still be in your project list after the import

      • Import URL Downloads a collection of songs directly from GitHub (used to load a collection of songs)..New songs you've made after exporting to .json-file will still be in your project list after the import
